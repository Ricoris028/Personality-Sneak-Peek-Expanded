the models contained within this folder are the original, unreleased models for Vaska's Lord of Lightning form.

i intially uncovered these models a few days before they were released. i added them to the previewer with an epilepsy warning, but later suffered from seizure due to exposure.
deeming them unsafe for viewing, i urged the developers to change the models. in response, they were adjusted, well before they were ever released to the public.
unfortunately, the revision was not enough for me to avoid the issue of seizure. i continued to fight for change, but no further adjustments were made.

normally, i would allow for legacy models of significance to be previewed here. but this, unfortunately, is of a type that i cannot support.
as of now, these models are being catalogued here so that they are not forever lost to time. download them as you please, but use them at your own risk.
